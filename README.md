I'm a generalist engineer who builds and deploys AI systems in production.

My main interest is in systems that can understand and continuously learn from the environments they operate in — moving beyond static models toward ones that update their internal representations through interaction.

This direction draws from ideas in predictive world models, such as those from [Yann LeCun](http://yann.lecun.com/)'s work on JEPA architectures.
